The Mathematics Department of King's College received a rating of 5 for both pure and applied mathematics in the recent national Research Assessment Exercise, which confirms the status of the Department as a centre of excellence in mathematics. It also has excellent links, especially at the postgraduate level, with other high-rated London Colleges.
