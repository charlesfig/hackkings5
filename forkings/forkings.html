What is the Senior Common Room?

The Senior Common Room (SCR) is both an organisation and a place. The SCR provides social facilities and light refreshments in a comfortable and relaxing environment. For a modest annual subscription, membership of the SCR is open to all staff of King's C ollege London and also to certain postdoctoral workers.


Where is the SCR located?

The Senior Common Room occupies a self-contained suite at the southern end of the Strand Campus Quadrangle, with views of the Somerset House terrace, the Embankment and the River. The suite includes two comfortably-furnished lounges with well-equipped ser very managed by a steward. The staff at the Reception Desk at the Strand Campus entrance and at the Central Desk in the Main Building will be able to direct you to the SCR suite.


What facilities and benefits does the SCR provide?

Daily Service

The Rooms are open for the use of subscribing members during normal College weekday working hours. A wide selection of daily papers and periodicals is provided. Morning and lunchtime coffee, sandwich lunches and afternoon tea are served at the following times (which may vary slightly during vacations):

morning coffee:10-30 - 11.30
lunch:12-30 - 14.00
afternoon tea:15.30 - 16.20

Prices are very competitive because part of the service cost is met from subscription income. A limited bar service including beers, wines and spirits is also available.

Other Activities

The SCR provides a range of occasional social opportunities for members including an Annual Dinner in the second semester, Christmas and Summer Parties, and wine tastings or auctions.

Other Benefits

Members may reserve all or part of the SCR suite for private functions, eg. book launches, receptions, birthday parties, in the evenings (after 18.00) or at weekends. At present, no charge is made for this facility unless the SCR Steward is required to pr ovide assistance, in which case service costs have to be met.

Joining the Senior Common Room

All members of staff of the College are eligible to join. Certain postdoctoral workers are also eligible. Staff working on campuses other than the Strand, and part-time staff pay a reduced subscription (50%). Current full-year rates are as follows:

Strand based full time staff: £18.00 pa
Other Campus'* and p/t staff: £9.00 pa

*Including Cornwall House and Waterloo Bridge House. Paid-up members of the Kensington Campus Staff Association are entitled to use the daily facilities of the Strand SCR without further subscription.

Subscriptions may be paid annually by cheque (made out to 'King's College Senior Common Room'); or deducted automatically from salary in two equal instalments (November and March).
